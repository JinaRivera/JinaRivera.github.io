Panic! at the Disco

By Jinamarie Rivera

Biography

Panic! at the Disco is an American rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada formed in 2004 by childhood friend Brendon Urie (the lead singer), Ryan Ross (sings and plays the guitar), Spencer Smith (plays the drums) and Brent Wilson (plays the bass). Panic! at the Disco now has 6 released albums. While sales started low with their first album back in 2005, the band soon announced their first headlining tour in March of 2006. Brent Wilson was kicked from the band and soon replaced by Jon Walker. The next year the band announced another "Beatles inspired" album, Pretty. Odd. Pretty. Odd. debuted at number two on the US billboard 200 chart.

Soon things started to change with the band. In 2009, Ryan Ross and Jon Walker announced they were leaving the band. Panic! at the Disco were now left with Brendon Urie, Spencer Smith & there touring bassist Dallon Weekes. The band re-entered the studio in early 2010 to start recording their 3rd album, Vices & Virtues, set to be released the next year.

After the Vices & Virtues tour, the band set on to start recording their 4th album that would be release on October 8th, 2013. This album was announced as "Too Weird to Live, Too Rare to Die!" Shortly before the band begain its first tour for the album, Spencer Smith wrote an open letter to the fans on how he is leaving to "continue fighting addiction. It wasn't until April 1, 2014 the Spencer has announced that he officially left the band. The band's 5th album "Death of a Bachelor" was released January 15, 2016. Bassist, Dallon Weekes offically announced he departure from the band on December 27, 2017.

Panic! at the Disco was then left with Brendon Urie. On March 19, 2018 introduced a new touring bassist, Nicole Row. June 22, 2018, Panic! at the Disco's 6th album, Pray for the Wicked was released. They are now on tour promoting the album.

    • January 2008 - The Pretty. Odd. Tour starts
    • July 6, 2009 - Jon Walker and Ryan Ross leaves the band.
    • March 22, 2011 - Third Album "Vice & Virtues" is released.
    • January 23, 2011 - The first leg out of three of the Vices & Virtues tour begins.
    • October 8, 2013 - Fourth Studio Album "Too Weird to Live, To Rare to Die" is released.
    • January 14, 2014 - Too Weird to Live, Too Rare to Die! Tour begins.
    • April 1, 2014 - Spencer Smith leaves the band.
    • January 15, 2016 - Fifth Studio album "Death of a Bachelor" is released.
    • February 24, 2017 - The Death of a Bachelor Tour begins.
    • March 19, 2018 - New Touring bassist, Nicole Row joins the band.
    • June 22, 2018 - Their 6th studio album "Pray for the Wicked" is released.
    • July 11, 2018 - The Pray for the Wicked tour begins. (Is set to conlude in March of 2019)
